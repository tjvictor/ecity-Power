Investors who have purchased our antique silver coins can trade/sell in these 3 channels:-

      1) Sell the antique silver coins via Auction Companies

      2) Trade the antique silver coins via our website (Trading Section)

      3) Return the antique silver coins to our company (after 12 months) for a fee of 6% annual rate* (Please take note – the warranty sticker must be intact)

In case of option 2 – where there is a change in the ownership of the antique silver coins, the seller and buyer can bring the antique silver coin (with the ownership contract) to our company and we can effect the transfer of ownership, there is a levy of 1% fee from buyer and seller individually.
